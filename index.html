ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Jaime, a graphics artist, and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet.

My interest in web development started back in 2022 so I decided to joined in KodeGo bootcamp. As an artist, my first love will always be crafting the visual.

Through KodeGo bootcamp. I was able to leverage my creative skills with an incredible web development toolkit, and now I spend most days branding, designing and building out web interfaces as a freelancer. but still exploring other technologies and frameworks that catch my interest!
